### Cycle: InnerGrow "Spending Tracker - Budget App" deep dive (2026-07-11)

**Sources used:**
- App Store listing: https://apps.apple.com/ph/app/spending-tracker-budget-app/id1525179720 (fetched 2026-07-11 — pricing, feature list, positioning, rating/review sentiment)
- 20 real in-app screenshots supplied by Gino: `artifacts/reference/IMG_9245.png` through `IMG_9265.png` (IMG_9256 not supplied) — used as ground truth over marketing copy, per Gino's instruction
- Pondo's own BRD (`artifacts/01-BRD.md`) as the baseline for "what Pondo has/lacks"

**Pricing & positioning (App Store listing, checked 2026-07-11):**
Free tier + in-app purchases: Monthly Premium ₱99, Yearly Premium ₱399, Lifetime Premium ₱599, **Family Premium ₱799**. Positioned as an all-in-one "Finance Planner, Money Manager." 4.8/5 stars across 9.6k ratings, #79 in Finance category. Praise: "intuitive interface," ease of use, goal-setting motivation, customizable categories. Noted complaint: "some of the more advanced features are locked behind a paywall."

**Cloud/pricing context relevant to Gino's cloud-transition decision:** InnerGrow's entire multi-device and multi-member value proposition depends on cloud sync ("access your data across all your iPhones and iPads seamlessly") and monetizes the household/shared-use-case specifically via a dedicated ₱799 **Family Premium** tier — i.e., "share your budget with your household" is treated as a premium, paid feature, not a free given. If Pondo moves to the cloud and adds multi-user/household sharing, there is a validated market precedent for treating that specifically as the premium differentiator/monetization hook, not a baseline free feature.

### Idea pool — Market/Competitive (InnerGrow cycle)

| ID | Opportunity | Why It Matters | Source |
|----|-------------|-----------------|--------|
| **M-1** | Household sharing with **granular per-member/per-account visibility controls**, not just per-member tagging. | **M-2** | **Per-counterparty lending/borrowing ledger** — itemize who owes what (e.g., "Mark — ₱5,000 outstanding," "Mom — ₱2,000 outstanding") within Pondo's existing Lent/Borrowed account types, rather than a single aggregate balance per account.
